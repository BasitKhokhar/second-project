Business & Management

Embark on a successful international career – earn a bachelor’s degree in business & management. At IU, you can study for an online BBA (Bachelor’s of Business Administration) degree from anywhere in the world, and start any time. Don’t let any restrictions or other obligations prevent you from achieving your goals. Join IU and study at your own pace, with maximum flexibility, and graduate with an internationally recognised business & management degree. Find out more about our online BBA programme, as well as our other business BA degrees, in the programme pages below.

Earn a high school diploma and a Bachelor's degree - at the same time!

Don't have a high school diploma? Have one but your grade average doesn't meet our admission requirements? We have a solution for you!

With A-Levels, you can earn a high school diploma and start your online Bachelor's degree at IU - at the same time.

Visit our A-Levels page for more details
