Data & IT

Pave your way to a successful international career in IT, with a bachelor online or on-location degree from IU, Germany’s largest university. We offer degree programmes in a wide range of in-demand professions, such as Data Science, Cyber Security, Robotics and Software Development. Pursue your interests, develop in-demand digital skills, focus on practical aspects and real-world case studies – and graduate with an IT bachelor online or on-location degree. Visit one of the programme pages below to find out more.

Earn a high school diploma and a Bachelor's degree - at the same time!

Don't have a high school diploma? Have one but your grade average doesn't meet our admission requirements? We have a solution for you!

With A-Levels, you can earn a high school diploma and start your online Bachelor's degree at IU - at the same time.

Visit our A-Levels page for more details
